Have you ever thought studying Virtually would be a reality?


Well since we are already here because of the pandemic(covid19)
which forced South African schools to go Virtual, from lower level grades to University
We might as well just discuss the impact of virtual reality on education.


The first problem I want to address is the way learners are educated. Learners are educated
using the old way of teaching whereby a teacher stands in front of learners and teach by writing on the board or using a textbook. For those learners that may have missed the class or lesson catching up becomes difficult as they usually have to catch up on their own or seek assistance from fellow learners. Most of these learners end up failing due to being unable to have the teacher physically present to teach them as it was done to the learners that were present in class that day. Another problem with the traditional way of learning is that it limits creativity ,it is boring and it leads to learners being uninterested and teachers being demotivated.
