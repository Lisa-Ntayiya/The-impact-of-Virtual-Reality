Traditional Education

To solve the first problem ,which is “traditional education” . I want to use virtual reality to change the way learners learn and teachers teach. I want learners to experience education in way that is more immersive and engaging . Virtual reality can take students from their desks to a world of wonder where they don’t just hear what they are being taught ,they see it and they can interact with it . Where they can be able to mix volatile chemicals without physically hurting themselves.

Teachers can be able to create conditions for exploring rather than providing knowledge that has been made ready. Students that miss a class or lesson they will be able to visit the lessons they have missed as if they were in class through virtual reality. Students can learn by actually performing tasks than just simply reading . Teachers and learners are able to create more memorable experience . Students are able to be more creative . Visual learners can finally do better academically. Virtual reality eliminates costs and distance barriers.
